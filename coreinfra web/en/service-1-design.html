1. Strategy & Design

Pre-planning and Detailed Engineering

Quality Control from the Source, Building Data Centers that Best Meet Your Needs

Data center construction is a complex system engineering process, and early strategic planning determines future success. We assist clients in accurately defining requirements in the initial stages and ensure that design schemes comply with local regulations and international standards.

📊

Preliminary Scheme Design & Capacity Planning

Based on cloud services and AI computing power needs, we conduct comprehensive planning for rack density, power, and cooling capacity. This ensures future scalability of the data center and provides precise Site Selection evaluation.

🏅

Certification Consulting Services

Targeting international standards (such as Uptime Institute Tier Standard certification), we provide professional design guidance and consulting to ensure alignment with high availability and fault tolerance standards right from the planning phase.

📐

Detailed to Complete Design Services

From Concept Design to Detailed Design, we deeply review cross-system interfaces such as MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing), HVAC, and ELV systems, eliminating potential design conflicts and risk points.
