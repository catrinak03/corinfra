4. Testing & Commissioning

Testing & Commissioning (Cx)

The Critical Defense Line to Verify a "Workable" Data Center

CoreInfra Systems insists that testing and commissioning should be involved early from the planning and design phase. We follow the internationally recognized Commissioning framework and use a rigorous five-level validation process to ensure the data center achieves maximum availability and stability before official production.

L1: Planning

Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) and Planning for Critical Equipment

L2: Site Acceptance

Site Acceptance Inspection and Static Installation Verification

L3: Pre-functional

Pre-functional Testing: Power-on and Basic Function Operations

L4: Functional Performance

Functional Performance Testing: Cross-system Linkage and Interlock Protection Logic Verification

L5: Integrated System

Integrated System Testing (IST): Simulated Full-Load Operation and Failover Drills

Professional Validation Technologies

🌡️ Thermal Simulation & Diagnosis

Using infrared thermal imagers and heat load simulators to conduct empirical analysis on the airflow organization and cooling efficiency of the server room.

⚡ Power System Stability

Conducting full-load power failure simulations for UPS, generators, and transfer switches (ATS/STS) to ensure seamless system switching under extreme conditions.

📋 Operational Handover Preparation

Ensuring all as-built drawings, test reports, and operation manuals are fully archived, and assisting the O&M team with site orientation.
