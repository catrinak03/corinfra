3. Construction & Installation

Construction & Installation

Standardized, Modular, and High-Quality Delivery

CoreInfra Systems has the capability to coordinate EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction) projects. Through rigorous PCM (Project Construction Management) processes, we simplify complex construction interfaces and ensure every detail of the data center infrastructure complies with international standards.

EPC One-Stop Integrated Delivery

Providing comprehensive services from shop drawing reviews to final acceptance:

  • International standardized delivery management
  • Cross-system interface integration (MEP, HVAC, ELV)
  • Construction quality management (QA/QC) and site inspections
  • Strict control of Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE)

PCM Professional Project Management

Acting as the client's most reliable representative and technical advisor:

  • Project schedule optimization and timeline control
  • Coordination for long-lead items arrival
  • Subcontractor management and construction conflict resolution
  • Project risk early warning and cost control

Our Core Value

"Not just completing a project, but delivering a stably operating asset."

We deeply understand cloud clients' strict requirements for Ready for Service (RFS) milestones. Through highly efficient integration and coordination, we help clients shorten the time-to-market.
