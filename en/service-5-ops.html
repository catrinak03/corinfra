5. Maintenance & Operations

Maintenance & Operations

Guardian of the Data Center Lifecycle

Delivery is not the end, but the beginning of operations. CoreInfra Systems helps clients establish standardized operations and maintenance systems, providing performance optimization and retrofit recommendations for existing facilities to ensure maximum asset value.

O&M Implementation & System Setup

  • Develop Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) and Emergency Operating Procedures (EOP)
  • Assist in CMMS/BMS operations management system implementation
  • Technical training and handover for the O&M team
  • Annual maintenance plan scheduling and spare parts management strategy

Existing Facility Optimization

For data centers that have been in production for years, we provide:

  • Retro-Commissioning: Identifying key points of performance degradation.
  • Energy Efficiency Optimization: Improving PUE and reducing energy costs.
  • Capacity Release: Releasing stranded power capacity through airflow optimization.

Facility Retrofit & Upgrades

Non-Disruptive System Retrofits

Upgrading power or cooling systems without impacting active server operations.

High-Density Transformation

Transforming traditional server rooms into spaces capable of supporting high-load racks for AI computing demands.

Sustainable Energy Transition

Introducing new energy storage systems or green power architectures to align with corporate ESG goals.
