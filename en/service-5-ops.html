Maintenance & Operations

Delivery is not the end — it's the beginning of operations. CoreInfra helps clients build a standardized O&M system, and provides performance optimization and retrofit recommendations for existing facilities to maximize asset value.

Lifecycle Guardian

The guardian of the data center's full lifecycle

From establishing systems to lifting the performance of existing facilities, we stay with the asset through every operating year.

O&M Onboarding & System Build

  • Developing Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) and Emergency Operating Procedures (EOP)
  • Assisting CMMS/BMS operations-management system rollout
  • O&M team technical training and handover
  • Annual maintenance planning and spare-parts strategy

Existing-Facility Optimization

For data centers in operation for years, we offer:

  • Retro-Commissioning: pinpointing the causes of declining performance.
  • Energy optimization: improving PUE and reducing energy cost.
  • Capacity release: unlocking trapped power capacity through airflow optimization.
