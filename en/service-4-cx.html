Commissioning & Handover

Testing & Commissioning (Cx)

CoreInfra insists that commissioning be engaged early, from the planning and design stage. Following an internationally authoritative Commissioning framework, our rigorous five-level verification ensures the data center reaches the highest availability and stability before going live.

01 Planning 02 Procurement 03 Construction 04 Commissioning 05 Operations

L1–L5 Framework

The critical line that proves a data center "works"

From factory testing to full heat-load operation, five levels of verification prove every system before it carries live workloads.

L1

Planning

Factory Witness Test (FAT) — planning and testing of critical equipment at the factory.

L2

Site Inspection

Site Acceptance Inspection — equipment delivery handover and static installation checks.

L3

Pre-functional

Pre-functional Testing — single-equipment energization and basic functional run tests.

L4

Functional

Functional Performance Testing — cross-system interaction and interlock-protection logic verification.

L5

Integrated Load

Integrated System Testing — simulated full-load operation and failover drills.

Verification Technology

Professional verification techniques

Thermal Simulation

Using infrared thermal imaging and heat-load simulation equipment to measure and analyze airflow organization and cooling efficiency in the hall.

Power System Stability

Full-load blackout simulations for UPS, generators, and transfer switches (ATS/STS), ensuring seamless switchover under extreme conditions.

Operations Handover

Ensuring as-built drawings, test reports, and operating manuals are fully archived, and helping the O&M team complete site orientation.
